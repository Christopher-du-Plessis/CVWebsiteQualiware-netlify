Work Experience

I have been working for a FinTech company called Sybrin Systems Limited for two and a half years. Sybrin offers a variety of banking related software processes to banks such as ABSA, NBC and Stanbic Bank. My current tile at Sybrin is "Software Application Support Engineer" and my role involves acting as a L2 escalation point for ABSA software issues that arise for services we offer them. As a L2 it is my duty to come up with permanent solutions to reocurring problems in the form of code alterations or code rebuilds. In addition to this my resposibilities may include the building of completely new services for ABSA that is covered withing their SLA. Some noteworthy achievements at Sybrin inlude:

  • Creation of CSV Windows Service for ABSA Tanzania, that will be used for Hadoop archival and analytical purposes
  • Creation of a C# Desktop application to renew Sybrin Licenses accross all ABSA Servers, there are over 155 and this used to be a manual process
  • Addition of Serial Numbers for the Remote Cheque Capture system in Kenya, speeding up processing at branches
  • Major optimizations done in SSIS packages. For Kenya Imaging and Workflow their was a daily package that took 4 hours to run, it now takes less than 30 minutes
  • Over 10 other user experience improvements for the Imaging and Workflow system in Kenya
  • Keeping the entire Tanzania Payments system (includes EFTs,Cheques and Foreign Cheques) running during skeleton staff times
  • Completing 34 ABSA compliance courses over the course of 2 years
  • Completing 11 Sybrin internal courses over the course of 2 years
